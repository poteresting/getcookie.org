# Privacy Policy

<p class="lead">
This Privacy Policy explains how GetCookie collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our Service. Please read this policy carefully.
</p>

*Last Updated: January 2026*

## 1. Introduction

GetCookie ("we," "us," or "our") operates a community recognition service through our Discord bot (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

By using the Service, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide and operate the Service. This includes:

### 2.1 Account and Identity Information

<DataTable>
| Data Type | Description | Purpose |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Discord User ID | Your unique Discord identifier | User identification and account association |
| Discord Server ID | The server where GetCookie is installed | Server-specific settings and recognition data |
| Username/Display Name | Your Discord display name | Displaying recognition messages and leaderboards |
</DataTable>

### 2.2 Recognition Data

<DataTable>
| Data Type | Storage | Notes |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| Recognition Events | Database | Who gave Cookies or reactions to whom |
| Cookie Balances | Database | Current Cookie holdings per user |
| Leaderboard Data | Database | Aggregated recognition statistics |
</DataTable>

### 2.3 Usage Information

- **Structured event logs** (e.g., commands used, recognition events)
- Error logs and debugging information
- Session information

**We do not store free-form message content beyond recognition messages you explicitly send through our commands.**

### 2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We practice data minimization. GetCookie does NOT intentionally collect or store:

- **Personal Identity Information:** We do not collect your real name, address, or phone number
- **Email Address:** We do not require or store email addresses
- **Payment Card Information:** We do not process credit cards or payment methods
- **Location Data:** We do not collect GPS or precise geolocation data
- **Discord Content Beyond Commands:** We only process bot commands and recognition reactions; we do not access your private messages or other Discord data

## 3. How We Collect Information

We collect information in the following ways:

- **Through Bot Commands:** When you use GetCookie commands to give or receive Cookies
- **From Discord:** We receive your Discord User ID and server information when you interact with our bot

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide the Service:** Process recognition events, maintain Cookie balances, display leaderboards
- **Improve the Service:** Debug issues, optimize performance, and enhance user experience
- **Communicate:** Send recognition notifications and important service updates
- **Ensure Security:** Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access
- **Comply with Legal Obligations:** Respond to legal requests and enforce our Terms of Service

## 5. Data Storage and Retention

### 5.1 Storage Location

User data is stored in secure databases.

### 5.2 Retention Period

We retain your data only as long as reasonably necessary:

- **Recognition Data:** Retained while your server uses GetCookie; can be deleted upon request
- **Event Logs:** Retained up to 30 days
- **Error Logs:** Up to 1 year

### 5.3 Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at support@getcookie.org. Upon a verified deletion request, we will delete:

- Your recognition history and Cookie balance
- Server-specific settings (if you are a server admin)

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@getcookie.org.
